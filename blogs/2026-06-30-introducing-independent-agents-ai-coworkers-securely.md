Glean introduced independent agents, autonomous AI coworkers that operate with their own identities, memory, and accountability within enterprise systems like Slack, Jira, and Teams. Its first agent, OnCall Assistant, helps engineers during escalations by automatically investigating issues and preparing solutions while staying transparent and traceable within governance frameworks.
